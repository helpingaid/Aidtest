Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kindorya. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our solidarity search engine.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Search Data: Your search queries and search history (stored locally)
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Cookie Data: Information stored in cookies as described in our cookie policy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our search services
  • Generate revenue through ethical advertising to fund humanitarian projects
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior
  • Personalize your experience on our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using our cookie settings tool. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Custom Search: To provide search functionality
  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements and generate revenue
  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Search counters and preferences are stored locally in your browser and can be deleted at any time.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at: info@kindorya.org

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
